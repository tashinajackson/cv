About

I work at the intersection of blockchain infrastructure operations and ecosystem coordination. My background spans running proof-of-stake nodes, building stakeholder relationships at industry events, and facilitating technical discussions for institutional and regulatory audiences.

I've moderated panels at international conferences, contributed to validator ecosystem programmes, and supported organisations in translating infrastructure work into clear, credible communication. I focus on building durable systems, maintaining operational reliability, and fostering trust across technical and non-technical stakeholders.

What DeFi Needs to Grow Up

Panel Moderation, Cosmoverse 2025

How I contribute

  • → Conference representation: Speaking, moderating and coordinating participation at industry events under organisational banners
  • → Partnership coordination: Supporting institutional relationships and stakeholder engagement across validator networks, governance bodies and ecosystem organisations
  • → Programme delivery: Designing and coordinating ecosystem initiatives including validator programmes, governance frameworks and grants coordination
  • → Institutional translation: Articulating technical infrastructure and governance work for regulatory, policy and institutional audiences
  • → Documentation and process: Establishing clear communication frameworks, stakeholder briefings and repeatable coordination systems

Working approach

  • Clarity: Structured communication, early identification of dependencies, proactive stakeholder alignment
  • Systems: Building repeatable processes and documentation rather than one-off interventions
  • Delivery: Outcome-focused work with clear timelines and transparent progress
